#### Tournament Overview

Gamefest 2019 will have a Rocket League tournament that is scheduled to occur **only on the second day, November 3<sup>rd</sup>**. The tournament details are as follows:

- 3v3, team-based
- Bring-your-own-computer
- Tournament held on second day, November 3<sup>rd</sup>

##### Competitive Structure

Each tournament round will use the **standard 3v3 game mode**, and the overall tournament is as follows:

- The first phase will be Swiss, which we'll use as seeding for a knockout single-elimination bracket.
- The Swiss matches will be best-of-3, and all bracket matches (except the finals match) will be best-of-3. The bracket finals will be best-of-5

#### Prizing

Prizing will be determined at the event by number of ticket-price entrants into each game's prize pool.

Each entrant's $5 entry fee (not including venue fee) goes towards the prize pool. In addition, the prize pool has a base amount that can increase depending on the number of entrants:

- Base prize pool: **$24**
- Increased to **$48** if there are 5+ teams competing
