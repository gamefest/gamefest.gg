#### Tournament Overview

Gamefest 2019 will have an CSGO tournament that will span **both days** and will feature the following:

- 5v5, team-based
- Bring-your-own-computer
- Tournament held on both days, with the finals on Day 2, November 3<sup>rd</sup>

##### Competitive Structure

Each tournament round will use the **standard competitive ruleset** with two different round structures:

- The group stage will use a round robin format
- Top 8 teams play in single elimination, best of 3 playoffs
