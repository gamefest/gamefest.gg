#### Tournament Overview

Gamefest 2021 will have a CSGO tournament that will span **both days** and will feature the following:

- 5v5, team-based
- Tournament held on both days, with the finals on Day 2, April 11<sup>rd</sup>

##### Competitive Structure

Each tournament round will use the **standard competitive ruleset** with two different round structures:

- The group stage will use a round robin format
- Top 8 teams play in single elimination, best of 3 playoffs

##### Available map pool:

- de_dust2

- de_mirage
- de_inferno

- de_overpass
- de_nuke

#### Prizing

Prizing will be determined at the event by number of ticket-price entrants into each game's prize pool.

Each entrant's $5 entry fee (not including venue fee) goes towards the prize pool. In addition, the prize pool has a base amount that can increase depending on the number of entrants:

- Base prize pool: **$125**
- Increased to **$250** if there are 5+ teams competing
