#### Tournament Overview

Gamefest 2021 will have an Overwatch tournament that will span **both days** and feature the following:

- 6v6, team-based
- Best of five tournament rounds
- Bring-your-own-computer
- Tournament held on both days, with the finals on Day 2, April 10<sup>th</sup>

##### Competitive Structure

Each tournament round will use the **standard Overwatch competitive ruleset** with **2/2/2 lock enabled**.

Only heroes and maps currently available in competitive play will be allowed.
