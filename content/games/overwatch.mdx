#### Tournament Overview

Gamefest 2019 will have an Overwatch tournament that will span **both days** and will feature the following:

- 6v6, team-based
- Best of five tournament rounds
- Bring-your-own-computer
- Tournament held on both days, with the finals on Day 2, November 3<sup>rd</sup>

##### Competitive Structure

Each tournament round will use the **standard Overwatch competitive ruleset** with **2/2/2 lock enabled**.
