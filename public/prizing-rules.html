Tournament Overview

  • 5v5, team-based
  • Captain's mode
  • Tournament styling consisting of group stage and playoff sections, played on Days 1 and 2 (October 27th & 28th), respectively (schedule permitting)
  • Bring-your-own-computer
  • Structure:

    • Day 2: Semifinals will be played between the finalists of each group and the runner-up of the other group
      • First and second place will be determined by a best-of-five grand finals
      • Third place will be decided by the winner of a best-of-three decider series
