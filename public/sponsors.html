Sponsors

Meet the organizations that make Gamefest possible. If you’d like to learn more about sponsorships, feel free to email us.

Are you a fan of eSports? The Collegiate Starleague is a place for college students across North America to share their competitive gaming passions. CSL started as a small group of dedicated lovers of Starcraft who came together to bring college and university students the gaming experience of a lifetime.

RESPAWN was born, a fully dedicated gaming division. Powered by OFM’s expertise and vast R&D program, RESPAWN is committed to developing accessible gaming furniture with no compromise on quality or features.

Hi-Rez Studios is an independent, privately held video-game developer based in Alpharetta, Georgia, United States. The company was established in 2005 by Erez Goren and Todd Harris, and is now one of the largest video-game studios in the southeast United States.

Skillshot builds community through esports. Skillshot operates online and offline competitive video game competitions, from grassroots amateur tournaments to professional global leagues and live arena events, supported by industry-leading video production and active community management.

Axis Replay is the first recreational technical center of its kind where business and gaming meet. Axis Replay is a 25,000 square foot facility launching in 2018 and is located in the Grant Park Beltline District in Atlanta, Georgia.

Do you love anime and video games, and need something to spice up your gaming den? Get your gaming props at Loot Cave Co today!

Cooler Master's mission is to deliver high-performance products built for today’s gamer.
